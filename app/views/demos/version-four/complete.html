What happens next

Belgium is likely to have additional controls.

Before moving the waste you must:

  • complete an Annex VII from and send with the waste
  • have a contract between both parties for the recovery of the waste
  • ensure the waste is dealt with in an environmentally sound manner
  • ensure waste is treated using techniques equivalent to those used within the European union

For more information and advice on international waste shipments contact us on Askshipments@environment-agency.gov.uk.

Forms and guidance
