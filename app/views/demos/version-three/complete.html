What happens next

This import is subject to notification controls.

Before moving the waste you must:

  • get permission from the regulatory authorities in England and Germany
  • have a financial guarantee in place to make sure money is available to return waste should something go wrong
  • have liability insurance in place
  • draw up a contract for the recovery of the waste
  • pay a fee to the Environment Agency

For more information and advice on international waste shipments contact us on Askshipments@environment-agency.gov.uk.
